About Me

In 2014, I graduated from the University of South Carolina with a Bachelors degree in Advertising. Soon after graduation I headed up to New York City for an internship with the advertising agency, BBDO. While there I worked with a small new business team on various pitches. Six months later I was ready to explore a new part of the country, the lovely midwest, so I moved to Chicago and took another agency job at Ogilvy and Mather. I stayed with the company for three years working in account management. During that time I uncovered an interest in coding and began to dabble on my own. I'm now attending the Northwestern Coding Bootcamp and pursuing a career as a web developer.

I live in Wrigleyville with my boyfriend of five years and dog of six. When I find myself with some free time, I enjoy reading, painting on the rare occassion when I have enough time, playing frisbee with the pup, Hiccup, and eating pizza.
